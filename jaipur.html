Hawa Mahal

Hawa Mahal, also known as “Palace of Breeze”, was built in 1799 as an extension to the Royal City Palace of Jaipur.It allows the royal ladies who at the time strictly observed “pardah”, to be able to watch any processions and activities on the street without being seen by the public.

  • Timings: Everyday – 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM
  • Famous For: Its unique attraction is its 953 windows that cover the lace-like walls
  • Built By: Pratap Singh of Jaipur, Lal Chand Ustad
  • Built In: 1799

Amber Fort

Amber Fort is a fort located in Amer, Rajasthan, India. It is also known as Sheesh-Mahal(Mirror Palace). With its large ramparts and series of gates and cobbled paths, the fort overlooks Maota Lake.The architecture of Amer Fort is a beautiful blend of both indigenous and Mughal styles.

  • Timings: Everyday - 8:00 am–5:30 pm, 6:30pm–9:15pm
  • Famous For: Its majestic beautiful architecture and rich history.
  • Built By: Raja Man Singh
  • Built In: 1589–1614

Jantar Mantar

The Jantar Mantar, Jaipur is a collection of 19 astronomical instruments. It features the world's largest stone sundial, and is a UNESCO World Heritage site. It is near City Palace and Hawa Mahal.

  • Timings: Everyday – 09:00 AM to 4:30 PM
  • Famous For: Its 14 geometric devices that measure time, track the location of stars and the Earth’s movement.
  • Built By: Sawai Jai Singh
  • Built In: 1734

Bhangarh Fort

The fort and its precincts are well preserved. Haunted for generations, nobody is permitted to enter the fort before dawn and after dusk. It's believed that whoever visits the place at night would not return by morning. Spirits are considered to roam at this fort at night, and several strange noises are heard too.

  • Timings: EveryDay – 10:00 am–05:00 pm
  • Famous For: The most haunted place of India because of a tantric called Singhia.
  • Built By: Raja Bhagwant Singh
  • Built In: 1573 AD

Jawahar Circle

Crowned as the largest highway circular park in all of Asia, the Jawahar Circle Park in Jaipur is embedded with a number of attractions. From splendid jogging track to musical fountain, kiosks, rose gardens and more, the garden has several amenities that make it an instant attraction among tourists.

  • Timings: EveryDay - 6 AM - 9 PM. Musical Fountain Timings: 7 PM - 7.30 PM.
  • Famous For: Its Musical Fountain that displays 290 different light effects.
  • Built By: Jaipur Development Authority
  • Built In: 2009
